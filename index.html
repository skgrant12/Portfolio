ABOUT ME

Born and raised in small-town Illinois, I came to Indianapolis to study music. I started my adult life as a music educator, and continue to use those creative problem-solving skills as a software developer.

I earned my Bachelor of Music Education from Butler University in 2018, teaching Orchestra for four years in Houston and Indianapolis. I was looking for an opportunity for growth, and my path led me to study Software Development at Eleven Fifty Academy, with my graduation expected in April 2022.

I currently teach private music lessons at Bach to Rock Fishersand have played the double bass professionally in Indianapolis since 2014.

I'm looking forward to and seeking new opportunities in the industry.
